Ramen

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ramen Noodles 2 packs
Chicken Broth 4 cups
Eggs 2
Green Onions 2 stalks
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp

Step 1: Boil eggs for 7 min and peel.

Step 2: Cook noodles.

Step 3: Heat broth, add soy sauce.

Step 4: Combine noodles, broth, eggs, green onions.

Source: Just One Cookbook
